The Story

Ka the Creator was said to ride a chariot of lightning across the sky when he created Erthos. He created all life and left behind his children "The Godlings" to watch over us with the promise of Ka's return.

Every Millenia, however, the Godlings power becomes too much for them to contain and raw creational power drips from them and touches the mortal world.

One man known simply as the Castor acquired this raw power and forged the Paragon, a mighty weapon that scares even The Godlings. It's power so immense it was broken into several pieces and scattered across the lands.

When the 5 become 1 the Gods will be undone!
This prophecy has echoed throughout the history of Erthos. Many Heroes, Wanderers, and even Tyrants have tried to search for the weapon of legend, but to no avail.

Only the Chosen Five will be able to weild the power of the Godlings.

What do you need?

Like most TTRPGs you will need just a few things to play:

Game-Master: a story teller who will run the interactions of the game. This includes setting scenes, presenting scenarios, interacting with players as NPCs, and working through the players challenges and defends.

Players: Everyone else is considered a Player. You will build a character within the Paragon rule-set and attempt to accomplish goals of a personal motivation and those laid out by the GM or Adventure.

Dice: A good set of Dice is one of the most important parts of any TTRPG.
A standard set of Dice shoud inlcude the following:

D4, D6, D8, 2 x D10, D12, and D20.

Pen and Paper: You will need a standard pen and paper to track your progress, take notes, and increase skills/abilties via the Character Page!

Additional Resources: The links located above in the Navigation Bar will take you to resources like additional information, descriptions, rules and even downloadable content!
