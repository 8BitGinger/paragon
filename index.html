What do you need?

Like most TTRPGs you will need just a few things to play:

A Game-Master: a story teller who will run the interactions of the game. This includes setting scenes, presenting scenarios, interacting with players as NPCs, and working through the players challenges and defends.

Players: Everyone else is considered a Player. You will build a character within the Paragon rule-set and attempt to accomplish goals of a personal motivation and those laid out by the GM or Adventure.

Dice: A good set of Dice is one of the most important parts of any TTRPG.
A standard set of Dice shoud inlcude the following:

D4, D6, D8, 2 x D10, D12, and D20.

Pen and Paper: You will need a standard pen and paper to track your progress, take notes, and increase skills/abilties via the Character Page!
